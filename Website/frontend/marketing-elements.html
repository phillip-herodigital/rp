Becoming a Stream Energy customer is easy. We’re committed to helping you find the energy services you need at affordable rates, and our customer service team stays with you through every step of the process. But that’s just the beginning.

When you switch providers, the energy that comes into your home doesn’t change—the poles and wires are still maintained by your local utility. But as a Stream Energy customer, you can feel confident knowing that you’re backed by a $6 billion company.

  • A company that has changed the lives of hundreds of thousands of people
  • A company dedicated to helping our environment through the promotion of clean, renewable sources including wind power and others
  • A company that has been recognized for devoting time, money and manpower to help the communities it serves
