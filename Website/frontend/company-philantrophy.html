Stream Energy isn’t just providing affordable electricity and reliable products. We believe in giving back, in helping to make the world a better place. We do this by donating to our local communities and various charities through a program we call Stream Cares.

We’re proud to spotlight our amazing partners, and share with you the importance of these relationships.

  • Captain Hope's Kids

    Texas-based Captain Hope's Kids is the only agency in Dallas that is devoted exclusively to providing critically needed items to homeless children.

  • Fuel Fund

    The Fuel Fund of Maryland provides financial relief to families who require assistance in paying for their household utility service.

  • Heating Energy Assistance Team

    Economically disadvantaged families in Georgia rely on the Heating Energy Assistance Team for assistance in paying their energy bills.

  • Ragan's Hope

    Ragan's HOPE is a Texas-based charity committed to helping parents across the nation whose children suffer from catastrophic illnesses or injuries providing financial, emotional and spiritual support.

  • Utility Emergency Services Fund

    Based in Pennsylvania, the Utility Emergency Services Fund helps low income families in Philadelphia pay for life essential services.
